Sustainability

The numbers don't lie. According to FastCompany.com, around 30% of online purchases are subsequently returned, much of which goes to landfill. In 2020, an estimated 2.9 million tons of returns were disposed of this way in the U.S. alone.

Without thoughtful and innovative solutions like Curiate, this number will keep growing. We belive Sustainability is the reponsibility of each corporation in the Fashion and apparel industry. We are here to drive that change forward.
